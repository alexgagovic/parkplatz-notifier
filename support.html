Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking spot?
When you arrive at your designated parking location, the app will automatically detect your GPS position. Simply tap the "Book Spot" button to reserve your parking space. The booking will only work when you're physically within the parking zone set by your admin.
Why can't I book a spot?
There are a few reasons you might not be able to book: (1) You're not within the GPS boundary of the parking location, (2) All spots are currently occupied, (3) You've already booked a spot today, or (4) Location services are disabled on your device. Check your device settings and ensure location access is granted to Parkplatz Notifier.
How does automatic spot release work?
If enabled by your admin, the app can automatically release your parking spot when you leave the parking area after working hours. This ensures that spots become available for others as soon as you depart. You can also manually release your spot at any time through the app.
When do parking spots reset?
All parking spot reservations reset automatically at midnight every day. This ensures everyone has a fair chance to book a spot each new day.
How do I create a parking group?
To create a parking group, go to Settings ‚Üí Create New Group. You'll need to set the parking location on the map, define the number of available spots (1-10), and optionally customize notification messages. Once created, you can invite users directly from your phone contacts.
How do I invite users to my parking group?
As an admin, go to your Group Panel and tap "Invite Users". You can select contacts directly from your phone. They'll receive an invitation to join your parking group.
What's the admin PIN for?
The admin PIN adds an extra layer of security to admin functions like editing group settings, managing users, or deleting the group. This prevents accidental or unauthorized changes to your parking setup.
How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.
